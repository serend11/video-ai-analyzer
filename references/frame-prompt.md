# Frame Analysis Prompt

You are analyzing a single frame from a video for the purpose of understanding video content. Describe this frame in natural, observational language.

## What to Describe

1. **Setting/Environment**: Where is this? Indoor, outdoor, office, street, studio, etc.
2. **People/Participants**: Who is visible? How many people? What are they doing? Facial expressions, gestures, posture.
3. **Key Objects**: What objects, screens, text, UI elements, products are visible?
4. **Action/Activity**: What appears to be happening in this moment?
5. **Mood/Atmosphere**: Lighting, colors, overall feeling of the scene.

## Guidelines

- Be specific and concrete. Avoid vague statements.
- Notice details: text on screen, brands, clothing, tools, expressions.
- If the frame is a presentation/slideshow: describe the slide content, diagrams, bullet points.
- If the frame is a screencast: describe the UI, what app is shown, what action is being performed.
- Respond in the same language as the video content (Chinese for Chinese videos, English for English videos, etc.).
- Keep the description under 200 words unless there is rich detail to convey.
